  32: ## Definition
  33: 
  34: This indicator aims to evaluate:
  35: - social constraints resulting from denigration and attacks on the press based on such issues as gender, class, ethnicity and religion;
  36: - cultural constraints, including pressure on journalists to not question certain bastions of power or influence or not cover certain issues because it would run counter to the prevailing culture in the country or territory.
  37: 
  38: Please refer to: https://rsf.org/en/methodology-used-compiling-world-press-freedom-index-2024?year=2024&data_type=general

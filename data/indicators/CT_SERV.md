## Definition

Number of direct container liner shipping services servicing the ports in an economy

## Methodology

The indicator is calculated using the shipping schedules in the MDS Transmodal Containership Databank indicating the number of container shipping services by year and quarter. For a given economy, the indicator is the average number of services across the four quarters of each year.

## Definition

Number of direct container liner shipping alliances servicing the ports in an economy. A shipping alliance (or maritime alliance) is an agreement between two or more shipping lines to manage operational and commercial activities. This can include vessel sharing to expand network coverage.

## Methodology

The indicator is calculated using the shipping schedules in the MDS Transmodal Containership Databank indicating the number of liner shipping service alliances in an economy by year and quarter. For a given economy, the indicator is the average number of alliances operating in the economy across four quarters of each year.

## Definition

Number of people in Phase 3 (Crisis) or worse (Emergency or Catastrophe/Famine) on the Integrated Food Security Phase Classification (IPC) Acute Food Insecurity Scale (IPC/CH Phase 3 or above).

## Methodology

The methodology is described in the resource available at https://www.ipcinfo.org/fileadmin/user_upload/ipcinfo/manual/IPC_Technical_Manual_3_Final.pdf. The same link is provided under methodology references.

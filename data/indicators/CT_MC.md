## Definition

Number of economies accessible through direct liner shipping services

## Methodology

The indicator is calculated using the shipping schedules in the MDS Transmodal Containership Databank indicating the ports and economies serviced by every liner shipping service by year and quarter. For a given economy, the number of unique economy partners is aggregated by year.

## Definition

Number of people in each phase of the Integrated Phase Classification (IPC) Acute Food Insecurity Classification. The classification contains five phases representing level of acute food insecurity:
Phase 1 - None/minimal
Phase 2 - Stressed
Phase 3 - Crisis
Phase 4 - Emergency
Phase 5 - Catastrophe/Famine
